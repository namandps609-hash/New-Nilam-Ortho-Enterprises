Who We Are

Bihar's Dedicated Ortho Supplier

New Nilam Ortho Enterprises is a specialised orthopaedic supply company based in Muzaffarpur, Bihar. We are the trusted source for hospitals, orthopaedic surgeons and medical clinics seeking premium implants and orthopaedic solutions.

We handle standard procurement as well as fully customised orders tailored to the exact requirements of each clinical case — ensuring every patient receives what they need, on time.

As an authorised collaboration partner of Meril, we supply only certified, quality-assured orthopaedic products sourced directly from one of India's most respected healthcare manufacturers.
